# The Window That Remembered

Yolanda kept a blue notebook under the left side of her pillow, where good ideas could find it without making too much noise.

On Friday morning, the notebook was open to a page she had never written. At the top, in handwriting almost like hers, were five words:

> Please finish the door.

There was no door in the room, only a narrow window above the desk. But when Yolanda touched the glass, the reflection blinked first.

Outside, a library was floating where the garden should have been. Its shelves leaned into the clouds, and every book on them was waiting for a title.
